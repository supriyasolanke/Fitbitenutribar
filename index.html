What is a Nutribar?


Nutri-bar is a cereal bar that has a unique combination of whole-wheat and oats topped up with dry fruits and nuts.

The Horlicks nutri -bar comes in three flavors - namely Choco Crispy , Cereal n Milk and Nuts n Raisins .

According to the label it provides 114 calories and 1.4 protein There are various other nutri bars,

protein bars and bars containing nuts that provide different amounts of calories fat, proteins and other essential nutrients .
